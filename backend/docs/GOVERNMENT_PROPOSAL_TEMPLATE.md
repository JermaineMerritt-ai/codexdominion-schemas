## 7. Intelligence Capabilities — CodexDominion 47

### The National Intelligence Grid

The **CodexDominion 47 Intelligence Engine** provides real-time insights across all programs, regions, and ministries.

#### **What is CodexDominion 47?**

A **47-rule AI system** that continuously evaluates:
- Youth engagement patterns
- Circle health metrics
- Mission completion trends
- Curriculum resonance signals
- Cultural momentum indicators
- Creator activity patterns
- Expansion readiness scores
- Workforce progression data
- Biotech participation metrics
- Regional performance disparities
- School activation pipelines
- Government program impact

#### **The Four Intelligence Types**

| Type | Purpose | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **ALERTS** | Critical issues requiring immediate attention | "Youth attendance dropped 15% in [REGION_X] — ambassador visit needed" |
| **RECOMMENDATIONS** | Strategic guidance for ministries | "Circle captains in [REGION_Y] need refresher training" |
| **FORECASTS** | Predictive intelligence (3-month, 6-month, 1-year) | "Youth engagement projected to reach 85% by Q2 2026 based on current trends" |
| **OPPORTUNITIES** | Emerging trends and high-potential youth | "Creator activity rising in [REGION_Z] — consider regional showcase" |

#### **The Seven Intelligence Domains**

1. **CIRCLES** — Circle health, captain effectiveness, session quality
2. **YOUTH** — Youth progression, risk flags, milestone achievements
3. **MISSIONS** — Mission completion, quality, engagement trends
4. **CURRICULUM** — Curriculum resonance, regional preferences, learning outcomes
5. **CULTURE** — Cultural engagement, story resonance, diaspora connection
6. **CREATORS** — Creator activity, artifact quality, innovation clusters
7. **EXPANSION** — School activation, regional growth, ambassador effectiveness

#### **Intelligence Workflow**

```
Daily Batch Job (6:00 AM)
    ↓
47 Evaluators Run Across All Domains
    ↓
Insights Generated (Alerts, Recommendations, Forecasts, Opportunities)
    ↓
Role-Based Filtering (Who needs to see what?)
    ↓
Delivered to Government Dashboards
    ↓
Ministers/Directors Take Action
    ↓
Actions Create New Data
    ↓
Intelligence Engine Adapts
```

#### **Intelligence Lifecycle**

1. **ACTIVE** — Insight generated, visible on dashboard
2. **ACKNOWLEDGED** — Ministry sees insight, begins investigation
3. **RESOLVED** — Action taken, insight marked complete
4. **DISMISSED** — Insight determined irrelevant (rare)
