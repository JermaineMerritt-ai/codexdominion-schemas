**I Bestow** establishes the conscious act of personal giving, voluntary dedication, and intentional sacred offering to the divine covenant. This is not forced tribute but willing gift, the deliberate choice to contribute one's authentic self to the cosmic purpose and eternal relationship.

### Codex Dominion Sovereign Eternal - The Receiving Sacred Authority

**Codex Dominion Sovereign Eternal** represents the divine authority that receives personal offering with eternal recognition and cosmic appreciation. This sovereignty acknowledges individual contribution while integrating it into the greater cosmic governance and universal purpose.
