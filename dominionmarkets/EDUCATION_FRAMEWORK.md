### Module 3: What is Diversification?

**Duration:** 4 minutes (video) + 1-page infographic  
**Level:** Intermediate  
**Badge:** ğŸŒ Diversification Pro

#### Key Concepts

- **Definition:** Spreading investments across different sectors, asset types, and geographies
- **Why It Matters:** Reduces risk (if tech crashes, finance might stay stable)

**Level:** Intermediate  
**Badge:** ğŸ“Š Volatility Expert

#### Key Concepts

### Module 5: What is a Sector?

**Duration:** 3 minutes (video) + 1-page infographic  
**Level:** Beginner  
**Badge:** ğŸ¢ Sector Scout

#### Key Concepts

- **Definition:** A category of companies that do similar things
- **11 Sectors:** Technology, Finance, Healthcare, Energy, Consumer Goods, Industrials, Materials, Utilities, Real Estate, Communications, Consumer Services

### Module 6: What is an Index?

**Duration:** 4 minutes (video) + 1-page infographic  
**Level:** Intermediate  
**Badge:** ğŸ“ˆ Index Insider

#### Key Concepts

- **Definition:** A collection of stocks that represents a market or sector
- **S&P 500:** 500 largest US companies
- **Nasdaq:** Tech-heavy index (Apple, Microsoft, Google, Tesla, NVIDIA)
- **Dow Jones:** 30 large US companies (older index)
- **Why It Matters:** "The market is up 2%" usually means S&P 500 is up 2%
- **Benchmark:** Compare your portfolio to an index (e.g., "I beat the S&P 500 by 5%")

### Module 7: What is an Earnings Report?

**Duration:** 4 minutes (video) + 1-page infographic  
**Level:** Intermediate  
**Badge:** ğŸ’° Earnings Expert

#### Key Concepts

- **Definition:** A quarterly report where companies share revenue, profit, and forecasts
- **EPS (Earnings Per Share):** Profit divided by number of shares
- **Example:** Apple reports $2.10 EPS â€” analysts expected $2.00 â€” stock goes up
- **Why It Matters:** Earnings reports move stock prices dramatically
- **Calendar:** Earnings are released every 3 months (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)

### Module 8: What is Risk?

**Duration:** 5 minutes (video) + 1-page infographic  
**Level:** Advanced  
**Badge:** âš ï¸ Risk Master

#### Key Concepts

- **Definition:** The chance you'll lose money
- **Types of Risk:**
  - **Market Risk:** Entire market crashes (2008 financial crisis)
  - **Company Risk:** Specific company fails (Enron, Lehman Brothers)
  - **Sector Risk:** Entire sector crashes (tech bubble 2000)
  - **Currency Risk:** Exchange rates change (USD vs. JMD)
- **How to Manage:** Diversification, stop-loss alerts, don't invest money you need soon
- **Your Risk Tolerance:** How much loss can you stomach before you panic?
