# Operational Engine

## Overview

The Dominion runs on clear, repeatable systems that turn intention into action and culture into continuity. The Operational Engine ensures every identity, circle, mission, and season moves with coherence.

## Core Systems Architecture

### Dashboards
Identity-aware command centers
- **Youth Dashboard**: Missions, circle schedule, portfolio
- **Creator Dashboard**: Content pipeline, revenue, audience
- **Captain Dashboard**: Circle management, mission tracking
- **Admin Dashboard**: System-wide metrics, content management

### Workflows
Structured processes for recurring tasks
- Identity Workflows (onboarding, progression)
- Creator Workflows (content, product, revenue)
- Youth Workflows (missions, reflections, portfolio)
- Leadership Workflows (council cycles, ambassador duties)
- Operational Workflows (dispatch, audits, resets)

### Templates
Standardized documents for consistency
- Curriculum templates
- Mission templates
- Reflection prompts
- Product templates
- Leadership documents
- Seasonal planning
- Audit checklists
- Communication templates

### Checklists
Operational clarity for recurring tasks
- Daily checklists (dispatch, monitoring)
- Weekly checklists (circle sessions, mission reviews)
- Seasonal checklists (resets, launches, audits)
- Mission checklists (creation, assignment, review)
- Creator checklists (content, product, engagement)
- Youth checklists (mission steps, reflections)
- Leadership checklists (training, ceremonies, reporting)

### Dispatch Cycles
Daily, weekly, seasonal rhythms
- **Dawn Dispatch**: Daily alignment message
- **Weekly Digest**: Progress, upcoming events, highlights
- **Seasonal Dispatch**: Season transitions, major announcements

### Economic Engines
Portfolio, markets, news systems
- Portfolio management for wealth building
- Market intelligence for creators
- News filtering for informed decisions
- Cultural alpha for strategic insights

### AI Advisors
Intelligence layers supporting decisions
- Mission recommendations based on identity
- Creator content suggestions
- Circle health monitoring
- Seasonal planning assistance

## Three Primary Cycles

### Daily Cycle
**Dawn Dispatch:**
- Sent every morning (6-8am local time)
- Identity-specific content
- Mission reminder
- Cultural reflection
- Single action focus

**Components:**
- Seasonal greeting (e.g., "Dawn greetings in the Season of Identity")
- Identity reminder (who you are today)
- Mission step (what to focus on)
- Cultural reflection (story/symbol/ritual)
- Closing encouragement

**Example:**
> Dawn greetings in the Season of Mastery, builders of the Dominion.
>
> Today, you are Youth—growing, learning, leading. You are becoming.
>
> This week's mission: Document one skill you've mastered this season.
>
> Cultural Reflection: The Flame burns brightest when tended daily. What small action will you take today to keep your own flame alive?
>
> Move with purpose. You are building a civilization.

### Weekly Cycle

**Circle Gatherings:**
- 60-90 minute structured sessions
- Opening ritual (5 min)
- Mission review (15 min)
- Curriculum lesson (30 min)
- Reflection sharing (20 min)
- Closing unity moment (5 min)

**Mission Reviews:**
- Captains review submissions
- Feedback provided within 48 hours
- Approved work added to portfolios
- Revisions requested with guidance

**Creator Analytics:**
- Weekly performance summaries
- Content recommendations
- Revenue tracking
- Audience insights

**Leadership Syncs:**
- Captains report to regional directors
- Ambassadors share outreach progress
- Council reviews system health
- Adjustments planned

### Seasonal Cycle (Quarterly)

**Vision Setting (Dawn):**
- Community gathers (physical or digital)
- New season introduced
- Seasonal goals shared
- Identity pathways outlined
- First missions assigned

**Mission Execution (Day):**
- Weekly missions delivered
- Curriculum modules released
- Creator challenges launched
- Circle sessions held
- Portfolios updated

**Reflection Gathering (Dusk):**
- Seasonal achievements reviewed
- Insights shared
- Challenges discussed
- Refinements planned
- Stories celebrated

**Renewal Ceremony (Night):**
- Season closing ritual
- Gratitude expressed
- Growth honored
- Next season previewed
- Community renewed

## The Dawn Dispatch

Daily heartbeat message aligning identity, mission, culture, operations, and focus.

**Purpose:**
- Start every day with clarity
- Reinforce identity
- Focus attention on one thing
- Connect to culture
- Build momentum

**Format:**
```
Subject: Dawn Dispatch | [Season Name] | [Date]

Dawn greetings, [Identity],

[Identity Reminder - 1 sentence]

[Mission Focus - 1 paragraph]

[Cultural Reflection - 1 question or story]

[Closing Encouragement - 1 sentence]

— The Dominion
```

**Delivery:**
- Email (6-8am local time)
- App notification (optional)
- Dashboard banner
- SMS (opt-in)

**Frequency:**
- Daily, Monday-Friday
- Seasonal pause during Night season (optional)

## Database Implementation

### MetricSnapshot Model (from schema.prisma)

```prisma
model MetricSnapshot {
  id            String     @id @default(uuid())
  metric_type   MetricType
  value         Int
  recorded_at   DateTime   @default(now())
}

enum MetricType {
  ACTIVE_YOUTH
  ACTIVE_CREATORS
  MISSIONS_COMPLETED
  ARTIFACTS_SUBMITTED
  CIRCLES_ACTIVE
  REGIONS_ACTIVE
  EVENTS_HELD
}
```

**Analytics Overview Endpoint:**
```typescript
GET /analytics/overview
Response: {
  active_youth: 1250,
  active_circles: 42,
  missions_completed_this_week: 387,
  regions_active: 8
}
```
