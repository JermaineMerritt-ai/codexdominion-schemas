## 4. The Ambassador's Expansion Sequence

Every new school or region follows this **7-step pattern**:

### Step 1 â€” Cultural Alignment

**Understand the school's identity, values, and youth culture.**

**Questions to Ask:**

### Step 3 â€” Captain Selection

**Identify 2-5 youth leaders who will run circles.**

**Captain Criteria:**

- Reliable and consistent
- Curious and open to learning
- Culturally aware
- Leadership potential (not necessarily loudest or most popular)

**Selection Process:**
- Ask principal/teachers for recommendations
- Interview candidates (10-15 minutes each)
- Select captains who embody Dominion values

**Outcome:** 2-5 captains committed to leading circles

**Time:** 2-3 hours (interviews + selection)

---

### Step 4 â€” Circle Activation

- Ambassador attends first circle (model facilitation)

- Youth experience: story, mission, creation

**Outcome:** First circle successfully launched

**Time:** 2 hours (training) + 1 hour (first circle)

### Step 5 â€” Mission & Story Launch
