## 4. Continuity Protocols by Scenario

### 4.1 System Failure Protocol

**Triggered by:**
- Store downtime
- Funnel errors
- App crashes

**Actions:**
1. Activate automated failover
2. Switch to backup hosting
3. Deploy emergency rollback
4. Notify national teams
5. Log incident for review

**Continuity Goal:** Restore functionality within minutes.

---

### 4.2 Infrastructure Failure Protocol

**Triggered by:**
- Payment gateway outage
- Database failure
- CDN disruption

**Actions:**
1. Switch to secondary gateway
2. Activate database replica
3. Route traffic through backup CDN
4. Notify customers if needed
5. Log incident

**Continuity Goal:** Maintain revenue flow.

---

### 4.3 Market Disruption Protocol

**Triggered by:**
- Economic downturn
- Platform algorithm change
- Competitor surge

**Actions:**
1. Shift to micro-products
2. Increase affiliate pushes
3. Deploy trend-aligned content
4. Adjust pricing
5. Launch emergency campaign

**Continuity Goal:** Stabilize revenue.

---

### 4.4 Compliance Disruption Protocol

**Triggered by:**
- Legal changes
- Cultural backlash
- Platform violations

**Actions:**
1. Pause affected content/products
2. Review compliance issue
3. Adjust messaging or product
4. Retrain creators/affiliates
5. Relaunch with alignment

**Continuity Goal:** Protect reputation and trust.

---

### 4.5 Operational Disruption Protocol

**Triggered by:**
- Team turnover
- Creator burnout
- Affiliate inactivity

**Actions:**
1. Redistribute responsibilities
2. Activate Action AI support
3. Recruit new creators/affiliates
4. Deploy training kits
5. Rebuild momentum

**Continuity Goal:** Maintain execution rhythm.

---

### 4.6 AI Disruption Protocol

**Triggered by:**
- Automation errors
- Unintended actions
- Model drift

**Actions:**
1. Disable automation
2. Revert recent changes
3. Switch to manual mode
4. Review logs
5. Re-enable with safeguards

**Continuity Goal:** Ensure safe, aligned AI behavior.
