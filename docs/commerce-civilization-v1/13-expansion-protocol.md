## Expansion Failure Response

**If expansion fails in new market:**

1. **Pause**: Stop all campaigns immediately
2. **Analyze**: Intelligence Review (Step 7) identifies failure cause
3. **Stabilize**: Activate Continuity Protocol, protect existing operations
4. **Decide**: Global Commerce Director chooses path:
   - **Pivot**: Adjust strategy and retry
   - **Pause**: Delay 6-12 months
   - **Exit**: Withdraw from market
5. **Document**: Update Expansion Protocol with learnings
6. **Review**: Annual expansion summit discusses failure lessons

**Failure is intelligence. Every failed expansion makes future expansions smarter.**
