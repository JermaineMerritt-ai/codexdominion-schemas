## Presentation Guidelines

- **Core Deck**: 15-20 minutes (Slides 1-25)
- **Q&A**: 10-15 minutes
- **Deep Dive**: 30-45 minutes (include appendix)

### Delivery Strategy

**Slides 1-5**: Hook and Problem
- Establish vision and urgency
- Define unique positioning
- Build narrative tension

**Slides 6-12**: Solution and Mechanics
- Show why now (convergence)
- Demonstrate economic engine
- Visualize flywheel effect

**Slides 13-19**: Infrastructure and Resilience
- Showcase civilization architecture
- Highlight defensibility
- Demonstrate operational maturity

**Slides 20-25**: Traction and Opportunity
- Present metrics and momentum
- Define investment ask
- Paint 100-year vision

**Color Palette**
- Primary: Deep blue (trust, intelligence)
- Secondary: Gold (youth, prosperity)
- Accent: Fire red (dominion flame)
- Background: Clean white or dark slate

**Typography**
- Headlines: Bold, modern sans-serif

- Emphasis: Strategic use of bold and color

**Imagery**
- Youth creating and building
- Global map with activation points
- Dashboard screenshots (Command Center, Intelligence)

- Network visualizations

- Growth trajectories (hockey stick)
