# Christian Wedding Planning Checklist - Content Brief

## Lead Magnet Overview
**Title**: Free 12-Month Christian Wedding Checklist
**Format**: 8-page PDF printable
**Target**: Engaged Christian couples (ages 22-35)

## Contents Checklist

### Page 1: Cover
- Title: "12-Month Christian Wedding Planning Checklist"
- Subtitle: "A Faith-Centered Guide to Your Special Day"
- Featured verse: "Love is patient, love is kind..." (1 Corinthians 13:4-7)
- CodexDominion branding

### Page 2-3: 12-Month Timeline
**12 Months Before**
- Set wedding date and budget
- Begin guest list
- Choose wedding party
- Scripture focus: Proverbs 3:5-6 (Trust in the Lord)

**10-11 Months Before**
- Book venue (ceremony & reception)
- Hire photographer/videographer
- Research caterers
- Scripture focus: Ecclesiastes 4:9-12 (Two are better than one)

**8-9 Months Before**
- Order wedding dress
- Book florist & decorator
- Arrange music/DJ
- Scripture focus: Song of Solomon 2:16 (My beloved is mine)

**6-7 Months Before**
- Send save-the-dates
- Plan honeymoon
- Register for gifts
- Scripture focus: Genesis 2:24 (United as one)

**4-5 Months Before**
- Order invitations
- Finalize ceremony details
- Book hair/makeup
- Scripture focus: Psalm 37:4 (Delight in the Lord)

**2-3 Months Before**
- Final dress fitting
- Send invitations
- Order rings
- Scripture focus: Mark 10:9 (What God has joined together)

**1 Month Before**
- Confirm final guest count
- Final vendor meetings
- Marriage license
- Scripture focus: Ephesians 5:25 (Love sacrificially)

**Week Before**
- Final preparations
- Rehearsal dinner
- Prayer time together
- Scripture focus: 1 John 4:19 (We love because He first loved us)
