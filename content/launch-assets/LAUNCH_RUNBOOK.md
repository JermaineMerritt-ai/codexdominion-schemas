## SECTION 8 ‚Äî CONTINGENCY PLANS

### **Technical Failures**

**Scenario: Marketplace Down**
- **Immediate:** Activate backup server
- **Communication:** Post status update within 5 min
- **Resolution:** DevOps lead coordinates fix
- **Recovery:** Full system check before reopening

**Scenario: Payment Gateway Failure**
- **Immediate:** Switch to backup processor
- **Communication:** Notify affected users
- **Resolution:** Work with provider
- **Compensation:** Extend promos if significant impact

**Scenario: Traffic Overload**
- **Immediate:** Scale infrastructure (auto-scaling)
- **Monitoring:** Watch response times
- **Communication:** Celebrate the problem (high demand!)
- **Resolution:** Increase capacity proactively

### **Content Failures**

**Scenario: Low Creator Participation**
- **Immediate:** Personal outreach to committed creators
- **Incentive:** Launch bonuses for early uploads
- **Communication:** Highlight early adopters
- **Resolution:** Extend pre-launch phase if needed

**Scenario: Low Youth Signups**
- **Immediate:** Boost youth marketing
- **Incentive:** First 100 signups get bonus
- **Communication:** Direct outreach to communities
- **Resolution:** Partner with youth organizations

**Scenario: Negative Social Feedback**
- **Immediate:** Assess validity of concerns
- **Response:** Address publicly with transparency
- **Action:** Fix legitimate issues quickly
- **Communication:** Show we're listening & improving
